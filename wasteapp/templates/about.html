Our Mission

WasteNot bridges the gap between waste producers and waste consumers. We're building a sustainable ecosystem where waste becomes a valuable resource — not a liability.

🚀

What We Do

Industries list their surplus waste materials; consumers request what they need. With role-based access, real-time dashboards, and seamless request management, supply meets demand effortlessly.

🤖

Smart Features

Our AI-based matching engine recommends the best waste suppliers based on user needs, quantity, and proximity. Analytical dashboards help users make confident, data-driven decisions.

♻️

Circular Economy Impact

By minimising landfill and optimising resource utilisation, WasteNot drives real environmental impact — reducing carbon footprint and supporting a truly circular economy.
